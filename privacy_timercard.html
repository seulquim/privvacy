Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how TimerCard ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name or email address) on our servers. The App is designed to function locally on your device.

However, for service improvement and advertisement, the following types of information may be collected automatically:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID).
  • Log Data: Information such as app crashes, system activity, and the date/time of your requests.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not store personal data on a centralized server, your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data related to the App simply by uninstalling the App. Any data processed by third-party services (like AdMob) is managed according to their respective retention policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

TimerCard does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
